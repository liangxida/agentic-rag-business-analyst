\# Business Context



This project analyzes retail sales and promotion performance across product categories, regions, and sales channels.



The business team wants to understand whether promotions generate meaningful incremental sales beyond organic demand growth. Raw sales growth alone is not sufficient because sales may increase due to seasonality, regional demand differences, or baseline customer demand.



\## Key Business Metrics



Promotion ROI measures how much incremental revenue is generated per unit of promotion cost.



Sales uplift measures the additional sales generated during promotion periods compared with estimated baseline sales.



Category performance compares sales, units sold, promotion usage, and uplift across product categories.



Anomaly detection helps identify unusual sales spikes, abnormal campaign performance, or potential data quality issues.



\## Business Questions



Example questions include:



\- Which product category had the highest sales uplift?

\- Which region had the strongest promotion ROI?

\- Did promotions improve sales beyond baseline demand?

\- Which campaign had the highest incremental revenue?

\- Are there abnormal sales spikes or suspicious campaign results?



\## Product Use Case



The system is designed as an AI-powered business analyst assistant. It combines structured KPI analysis with retrieval-augmented generation so business users can ask natural language questions and receive data-backed explanations.
